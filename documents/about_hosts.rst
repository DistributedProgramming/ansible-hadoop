About hosts
==============
We have two types of Ansible hosts sample.

hosts.large_sample:

 * NameNode: 2 nodes
 * Zookeeper and JournalNode: 3 nodes
 * ResourceManager: 2 nodes
 * Client: 1 node
 * Slave: 10 nodes

hosts.medium_sample:

 * Master Services(NameNode, Zookeeper, JournalNode and ResourceManager): 3 nodes
 * Client: 1 node
 * Slave: 5 nodes
